About

GroupStreamer is a music recommender system for parties and gatherings: it picks out the best music on your Android device given the people around you.

It's a new project from EPFL's Laboratory for Communications and Applications 4 whose purpose is to investigate recommender systems in mobile settings.

View details »

Technology

GroupStreamer analyzes each user's music library to automatically determine musical tastes.

Individual preferences are then combined in such a way that everyone in the group is as satisfied as possible.
